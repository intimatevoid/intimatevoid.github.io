I'm part of the Crooked Russian Cam podcast network! I appear primarily on two of our shows, Jemjammer and I Will Fight You.

All our shows can be listened wherever you get your podcasts!
We recommend searching for Crooked Russian Cam on a podcatcher like Podcast Addict or Pocket Casts, but you can also find our episodes on YouTube and Spotify.

Jemjammer

"Glorious glittery gay garbage."

This is my main show! Primarily, I'm the Dungeon Master for Jemjammer.

Jemjammer is a Dungeons and Dragons actual play show, set in the spacefaring setting Spelljammer. The show's been running since 2018, and I've been the Dungeon Master since 2023.

Our show has a thematic focus on finding your own family, feeling big messy queer feelings, and loving deeply and unapologetically in the face of a cold uncaring universe. We explore these themes through the lens of a galaxy-spanning conspiracy to turn all of creation to a state of nihilistic undeath. It's silly, it's sexy, it's sincere, and we don't take ourselves too seriously.

As we're well past 100 episodes, my co-host Annie and I created a recap episode to help get new listeners up to speed, which you can listen to on our website here or on our YouTube. But also, I think my friends are pretty cool, and would love it if you listened from the beginning!

I Will Fight You: Wolfpäc

"The most movie per movie, every movie."

I also occasionally appear on I Will Fight You, a pop culture opinion talk show. Every few episodes, Lucas Brown and I interrupt the regular IWFY feed with Annie Craton to bring you the Wolfpäc! Together, we deep-dive into films that are The Most Movie Per Movie, anything too jam-packed with themselves for the regular IWFY feed.

Date Me, Dammit!

"This is a woman who just stuffed some oranges in her shirt."

I appeared on one arc of Date Me, Dammit! hosted by Maq Weaver. Maq likes to play terrible otome games, and she likes to inflict that suffering on her CRC co-hosts and on you, our dear listeners.
